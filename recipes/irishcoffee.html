Irish Coffee

Description

Irish coffee is a hot caffeinated cocktail consisting of coffee, Irish cream liqueur, and Irish whiskey. It's topped with whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish cream liqueur (Baileys is a good choice!)
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish whiskey (Jameson is a good choice!)
  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon whipped cream
  • 1 dash ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Combine Irish cream liqueur and Irish whiskey in a glass mug; pour in hot brewed coffee. Top with whipped cream and a dash of nutmeg.

Recipe Tip

Preheat the mug or cup to help keep the drink warm for longer. While you're brewing the coffee, pour hot (not boiling!) water into the mug. Discard the water just before mixing up the Irish coffee.
